    Define a Limit

    The limit-function helps you to limit the amount of data you retrieve from Kyligence Enterprise. Especially for prototyping it could help you to save time and ressources. If you want to remove the limit-function just delete "LIMIT VALUE" from your script.

    Table and Column Details

    On the left side you can discover your available tables. This table helps you to get a better understandig how many rows of data your tables are containing. On the right side you can discover the column view. It shows you how many distinct values your columns contain and which data-type they have.
